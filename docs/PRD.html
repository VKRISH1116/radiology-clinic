How this differs from the URD. The URD said what users need. This PRD decides what we build: it draws the MVP line, groups needs into features (each tagged with the URD IDs it satisfies — UR-x-nn), and gives every feature acceptance criteria in Given/When/Then form. Those criteria become the test cases in Phase 7 — so "done" is defined here, not guessed later.

1Vision & goals

Vision. Give a single-location ultrasound clinic a simple web app that moves booking, registration, report delivery, and referral-payout tracking off phone-and-paper and online — safely and with clear consent.

Product goals (from URD objectives O1–O4):

  • Let patients self-serve booking and get 24/7 secure access to reports.
  • Cut front-desk phone/paper workload with a fast daily workflow.
  • Make referral payouts accurate, transparent, and auditable.
  • Protect patient data with explicit consent and role-based access.

4Features & acceptance criteria

Seven feature epics. Each lists the URD needs it satisfies and a few acceptance criteria (AC) written as Given/When/Then — deliberately testable.

F1Accounts & authentication (UR-P-01, P-09, A-07)

Email + password login with three roles. A seeded first admin creates staff/admin accounts; patients self-register.

AC-F1-1Given a visitor on the register page, When they submit a unique email + valid password + accept consent, Then a patient account is created and they are logged in.
AC-F1-2Given a user with role PATIENT, When they call a staff-only page/endpoint, Then access is denied (403).
AC-F1-3Given a registered user, When they request a password reset, Then a time-limited reset link is emailed and can set a new password once.

F2Appointment booking & slots (UR-P-02, P-04, P-08, S-07, S-08, NFR-08)

Patients pick one or more studies and an available slot. Staff define slot capacity. Appointments can be cancelled/rescheduled. No double-booking.

Pick studies→Choose available slot→Confirm→Status: Booked
AC-F2-1Given a slot at capacity, When a patient tries to book it, Then the slot is not offered / booking is rejected (NFR-08).
AC-F2-2Given a patient booking two studies (e.g. NT ₹1,000 + Doppler ₹1,000), When they confirm, Then one appointment is created with both studies and an indicative bill of ₹2,000.
AC-F2-3Given a booked, not-yet-started appointment, When the patient reschedules to another available slot, Then the old slot is freed and the new one reserved.

F3Registration & consent (UR-P-03, S-02)

Collect patient details and capture explicit, timestamped consent before storing data. Staff can register walk-ins.

AC-F3-1Given the registration form, When the consent box is unticked, Then submission is blocked and no data is stored.
AC-F3-2Given consent is given, When the patient registers, Then a consent record with timestamp and version is saved (NFR-02).

F4Reports (UR-S-05, P-05, NFR-01, NFR-03)

Staff upload a report file (PDF) to a visit; the owning patient downloads it after login. Access is logged.

AC-F4-1Given a completed visit with an uploaded report, When the owning patient opens the visit, Then a Download button appears and returns the file.
AC-F4-2Given a report belonging to patient A, When patient B requests it, Then access is denied and the attempt is logged.

F5Visit, billing & status (UR-S-03, S-04, S-06)

Staff tag the referring doctor, confirm/adjust the billed amount (defaults to sum of study prices), and move status Booked → In-progress → Completed.

AC-F5-1Given a visit with studies totalling ₹2,000, When staff open billing, Then the billed amount defaults to ₹2,000 and is editable.
AC-F5-2Given a visit, When staff set status to Completed, Then it appears as completed to the patient and is eligible for referral calculation.

F6Referring doctors & referral engine (UR-A-02, A-03, A-04, A-05, A-06)

Admin manages doctors and referral rules. The engine computes each visit's referral from the bill using the most-specific matching rule (default 20%). Running totals and payout status per doctor.

AC-F6-1Given only the default rule (20%), When a ₹2,000 visit referred by Dr. Rao completes, Then the referral is ₹400.
AC-F6-2Given a doctor rule "Dr. Rao = 25%" plus the default, When the same visit completes, Then the most-specific rule wins and the referral is ₹500.
AC-F6-3Given several completed visits for Dr. Rao, When admin opens his summary, Then it shows patients referred, total billed, total referral owed, and paid/pending.

F7Admin dashboard (UR-A-01, A-06)

At-a-glance activity: today's appointments, patient/report counts, and top referring doctors.

AC-F7-1Given the admin dashboard, When it loads, Then it shows counts of today's appointments, total patients, reports pending vs delivered, and a top-5 referrers list.

F8Service catalogue management (admin, PRD-added)

Admin can add, edit, and deactivate ultrasound services and set prices in-app. To keep past bills correct, a study's price is snapshotted onto each appointment at booking time (BR-9).

AC-F8-1Given an admin on Manage Services, When they change a study's price, Then new bookings use the new price while existing appointments keep their snapshotted price.
AC-F8-2Given a deactivated service, When a patient books, Then it is not offered in the study list.

5Business rules

  • BR-1 Billing: A visit's indicative bill = sum of its studies' catalogue prices; staff may override the final billed amount.
  • BR-2 Referral amount: referral = billed amount × applicable rule's percentage.
  • BR-3 Rule precedence: most specific wins — doctor+scan-type > doctor > scan-type > amount-range > clinic default (20%).
  • BR-4 Referral eligibility: a referral is only counted once a visit is Completed and has a referring doctor.
  • BR-5 Slot capacity: a slot cannot hold more appointments than its capacity (NFR-08).
  • BR-6 Consent gate: no personal data is stored without a recorded consent (NFR-02).
  • BR-7 Report access: a report is visible only to its owning patient and authorised staff/admin (NFR-01).
  • BR-8 Slots: appointment slots are 15 minutes, 09:00–21:00 daily (48 slots/day); per-slot capacity is configurable by staff (UR-S-08).
  • BR-9 Price snapshot: each study's price is copied onto the appointment at booking; later catalogue-price edits never change existing bills.

8Success metrics

  • % of appointments booked online (vs walk-in/phone) — adoption.
  • Median time from "report uploaded" to "patient downloaded" — access speed.
  • Referral total accuracy: system total matches a manual spot-check — trust.
  • Front-desk time per visit — efficiency (O1).
